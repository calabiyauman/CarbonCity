Pump watch

This is a quick application I developed in about 4 hours on a slow day. The User inputs data to the form and submits it to the database. The Idea was to use this in the Oil and gas industry for water transfer operators to track pump vitals. The last 6 entries are retrieved from the database. This uses the firebase.js API , a RESTful, NoSQL DB.
